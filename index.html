How It All Began

Our Story

♥
"

Love found us when we least expected it, but looking back, it feels as though our hearts had known all along.

Four years ago, we met as colleagues, never imagining that a simple workplace friendship would one day become the love story of our lives. What started with shared shifts soon became coffee breaks, chai breaks, food adventures, endless conversations, and countless moments of laughter. Somewhere along the way, spending time together became the best part of our day.

As our friendship grew, so did our place in each other's lives. We became each other's comfort, biggest cheerleaders, and the person we could always count on. Then life took us in different directions. Miles apart and living in different corners of the world, we could have drifted away. Instead, we grew closer. Through endless video calls, late-night conversations, and simply being there for each other when the world was asleep, distance only strengthened what we shared.

There was no single moment when love arrived. It grew quietly through friendship, trust, and the comfort of knowing we had found home in each other. Looking back, it feels as though love had been there all along — in every coffee break, every chai break, every shared meal, every laugh, and every conversation that brought us closer.

Today, as we begin this new chapter, we do so with hearts full of gratitude.

Because after all these years, the most beautiful part of our story remains the same: before we fell in love, we were best friends.

✦
Reuben & Joshina
